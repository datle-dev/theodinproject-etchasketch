Etch-a-Sketch

Current grid size: 10 x 10

Instructions:

  • Hover your mouse over the canvas to paint in black
  • Enable "Random Color" to paint a random RGB value
  • Enable "Opacity Mode" to paint by progressively adding opacity
    • For blank pixels, color (random or black) is added at 10% opacity
    • For existing colors, RGB is preserved and only opacity is added in 10% increments
  • Click "Start new canvas" to clear and resize the canvas
  • Click "Clear canvas" to clear the canvas, but maintain the current grid size
